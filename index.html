How do we measure engagement?

A Simplistic Approach: Active Users

- Our most engaged clients log in nearly every single day, multiple times a day. Expressed in the data they log in at least 22 times a month on at least different 22 days. They are Daily Active Users (DAUs).

- Some clients are moderately engaged and log in more sporadically. They log in less than 22 times but more than 4 times per month. They are Weekly Active Users (WAUs).

- Our least engaged active clients log in less than 4 times per month. Let's call them monthly users or Monthly Active Users (MAUs).
